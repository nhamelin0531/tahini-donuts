Tahini Donuts

Usage of Tahini in Donuts

Tahini is a very versatile ingredient and condiment being used in salads, meats and many desserts as well, including the donut. But why put tahini in a donut? Since tahini is a plant-based ingredient it can be used to create healthier and vegan donuts. And for those who want to be more adventurous it can add new flavours to your donut that you have yet to discover. So why not try some of the donuts listed here and see what you think?

Section 2

Because tahini is mainly used in the filling or icing of a donut there is a lot you can do. Like regular donuts there are many types of donuts made using tahini. There are salted, maple glazed, chocolate and many other types. These donuts can also be healthier depending on the other ingredients used in the donut. This makes sure a donut made with tahini can both be made healthier and given a different taste than a regular donut.

Section 3

The leeway that donuts made with tahini gives you with each process can make it much easier to enjoy a vegan dessert. This is because tahini is a plant-based ingredient. There are many different recipes out there for vegan donuts, so if you want to try a vegan donut, why not use tahini?

section 4

Whether you're wanting to try a vegan donut, wanting to try a new flavour for your donut or just have tahini lying around, tahini donuts are something you should try for yourself. With the donuts listed above and the recipes along with them, you can make, or even buy yourself a treat.
